DAILU COMMUNION

Sunday, August 23rd
Topic: Koinonia I
Passage: 2 Corinthians 13:5-14
Focus: 2 Corinthians 13:14

"The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, and the love of God, and the communion (Koinonia) of the Holy Ghost, be with you all. Amen."

SADOZ as a ministry has one goal amongst others: to bring everyone to the reality of communing with the Holy Spirit. What we are saying is, you can hear the voice of God the Holy Spirit and communicate with Him on a regular basis. We have been in the business of bringing all into this experience for some years and several people have experienced it. Some of us have never had the experience ourselves until we joined SADOZ and I believe all that desire to share the experience will have it through this series.

...

The word Koinonia is actually a Greek word used in 2 Corinthians 13:14: The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, and the love of God, and the communion of the Holy Ghost, be with you all. Amen. When we check for the meaning of the word translated communion in English, we realized it had several meanings among which are: Fellowship, Communion, Community, Partnership, Comradeship, Association & Intimacy. So, in reading 2Cor.13:14 we can therefore say, may the intimacy of the Holy Spirit be with you or may the association of the Holy Spirit be with you and so on. Let's see how the Amplified writes it: the presence and fellowship (the communion and sharing together, and participation) in the Holy Spirit be with you all. Amen (so be it).

God the Holy Spirit want us to have a Koinonia experience with Him. He wants us to have an intimate association and fellowship with Him. And there is no association that can exist without the art of communication. They say communication is the soul of all relationship. So, we must commune with Him. I believe you wish to commune with the Holy Spirit as well. We shall outline the necessary steps from the following week.

May the communion of the Holy Spirit be with you.
